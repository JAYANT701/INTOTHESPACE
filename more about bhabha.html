Homi Bhabha

FRS was an Indian nuclear physicist who is widely credited as the "father of the Indian nuclear programme".

Born: 30 October 1909, Mumbai

Died: 24 January 1966, Mont Blanc

Siblings: Jamshed Bhabha

Parents: Jehangir Hormusji Bhabha, Meheren Bhabha

Education: The Institute of Science, Mumbai (1927),

Full name: Homi Jehangir Bhabha

Academic advisors: Paul Dirac, Ralph H. Fowler
