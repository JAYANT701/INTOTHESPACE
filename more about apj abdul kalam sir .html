A.P.J Abdul Kalam

Abdul Kalam BR was an Indian aerospace scientist and statesman who served as the 11th president of India from 2002 to 2007.

He was born and raised in Rameswaram, Tamil Nadu and studied physics and aerospace engineering.

Born: 15 October 1931, Rameswaram

Died: 27 July 2015, Shillong

Full name: Avul Pakir Jainulabdeen Abdul Kalam

Awards: Bharat Ratna, Veer Savarkar Award, Padma Bhushan

Education: Madras Institute of Technology, Anna University (1955–1957)

Previous offices: President of India (2002–2007), Principal Scientific Adviser to the Government of India (1999–2001)
